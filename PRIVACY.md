# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026

MobileClaw is built with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device.

## What Data Is Stored

- **Conversation history** — Stored locally in a Room database on your device
- **API tokens** — Stored locally in Android EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256-GCM, backed by Android Keystore)
- **User preferences** — Stored locally in Android DataStore
- **Skills** — Stored locally in app internal storage

## What Is Sent to Third Parties

- **AI provider API calls** — Your messages are sent to the AI provider you configure (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.) to generate responses. MobileClaw does not control how these providers handle your data. Refer to their privacy policies.
- **DuckDuckGo** — Web search queries are sent to DuckDuckGo's Instant Answer API when using the web search tool.
- **No other data is sent anywhere.**

## What Is NOT Collected

- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting
- No advertising identifiers
- No location tracking (unless you explicitly use the navigation tool)
- No background data collection

## Accessibility Service

MobileClaw's Accessibility Service reads the screen content of other apps to enable AI-controlled automation. This data is:
- Processed locally on your device
- Sent to your configured AI provider only when you initiate a task
- Never stored persistently (screen content is ephemeral)

## Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time:
- Settings > Data & Storage > Clear Conversation History
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
