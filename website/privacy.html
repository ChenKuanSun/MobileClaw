Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Affiora, Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates MobileClaw ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Our Core Principle

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. MobileClaw is designed to run entirely on your device. There is no Affiora backend server that receives your data.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your Android device and never leaves it:

  • Conversations and messages: Stored in a local Room database on your device.
  • API keys: Encrypted using AES-256-GCM backed by the Android Keystore system, stored in EncryptedSharedPreferences. We never have access to your API keys.
  • User preferences: App settings stored locally via Android DataStore.
  • Skill files: Custom skills you create or install are stored locally on your device.
  • Scheduled tasks: Cron task configurations are stored locally.

3. Third-Party AI Providers

When you use MobileClaw, your messages are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you have configured (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.). We are not an intermediary in this communication.

  • Your messages go directly from your device to the AI provider's API.
  • We do not proxy, log, or inspect these communications.
  • Each AI provider has its own privacy policy and data handling practices. We encourage you to review them.
  • You provide your own API key — we never see or store it on our systems.

4. Android Permissions

MobileClaw requests various Android permissions (SMS, contacts, calendar, camera, accessibility, notifications, etc.) to enable its tools. These permissions:

  • Are requested only when you enable specific tools.
  • Can be granted or revoked at any time through Android system settings.
  • Are used solely to execute on-device tool operations as instructed by you.
  • Do not result in any data being sent to our servers.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, telemetry, or advertising SDKs. The App contains no code that reports usage data, crash reports, or behavioral data to us or any third party.

6. Distribution

The App is distributed as a direct APK download from GitHub. We do not use any app store for distribution. No payment information is collected or processed by us.

7. Data Security

  • API keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using keys managed by the Android Keystore.
  • Local database content is protected by Android's application sandboxing.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers, so there is no server-side data to breach.

8. Children's Privacy

MobileClaw is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
